info OVERVIEW

SkillUp.AI is an AI-guided career intelligence web application designed to support technical career growth. It combines user profile data, career discovery, real-world job analysis, and actionable checklists into one guided experience. Through structured pathways and milestone tracking, SkillUp.AI helps users close the gap between their current skills and industry requirements.

report_problem PROBLEM

Many students, recent graduates, and tech professionals struggle to identify the specific skills required for their target roles. Furthermore, translating complex job requirements into actionable learning plans remains a significant challenge. SkillUP.AI addresses this by converting abstract job market demands into clear, actionable proof points, seamlessly connecting career discovery with tangible execution.
